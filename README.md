# IoT & Artificial Intelligence Integration – A Case Study

## Overview

This project presents a case study on the integration of the Internet of Things (IoT) and Artificial Intelligence (AI). It explains how IoT devices collect real-time data, how AI analyzes the collected information, and how both technologies work together to build intelligent and automated systems.

## Objectives

- Understand the concepts of IoT and Artificial Intelligence.
- Learn how IoT and AI work together.
- Explore real-world applications in different industries.
- Study the benefits and challenges of IoT and AI integration.
- Understand the future scope of intelligent IoT systems.

## Conclusion

The integration of IoT and Artificial Intelligence enables smart systems that can collect, analyze, and respond to data automatically. This technology improves efficiency, supports better decision-making, and has applications in healthcare, agriculture, transportation, industries, and smart cities. As technology continues to evolve, IoT and AI will play an even greater role in building intelligent and connected solutions.
